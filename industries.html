Financial Services

Intuition has provided award winning educational programs to the financial markets for over 25 years. Our market leading library of financial tutorials (Intuition Know-How) contain in excess of 430 hours of learning (in multiple languages) and helps over 1.5 million finance professionals keep their skills up to date each year.

Intuition Know-How’s coverage includes introductory to advanced finance topics on all areas of financial markets, including Equities & Fixed Income, Derivatives, Risk Management, Financial Analysis & Valuation and Regulation & Compliance.

Intuition also distributes a free monthly newsletter which focuses on ‘What’s Trending in the Financial Markets’. The newsletter highlights which areas of the Know-How library are relevant to each story.

Intuition’s eLearning courses can also be used for continuing education and examination preparation. Please contact your local Intuition office for further details on NASBA, ACI Exams, CFA and other professional bodies.

Life Sciences

Intuition is a global leader in the provision of knowledge solutions to the global pharma, biotech and medical device industries. Our proven multi-channel solutions are in use in areas across compliance and ethics to product and sales training. With a global presence, Intuition has an international team of experts ready to meet your learning requirements.

Intuition creates custom eLearning courses to cover a full range of regulatory and compliance issues, from the Sunshine Act to Corporate Integrity Agreements. With a deep understanding of what it takes to meet the stringent content and tracking needs of the regulator, Intuition has also developed and hosted customized web portals for organization’s third party and agency CIA training requirements.

Our award winning mobile application is also used by Life Sciences organizations to push content to senior management and field-based sales people while our knowledge management platform is used by global medical experts to collaborate and share information.

Public Sector & Health

Governments and public sector organizations are under ever-increasing pressure to deliver more with less. Demands to improve efficiency of service, coupled with the need to communicate to a multi-cultural and often global audience requires a re-imagining of service delivery.

As an established knowledge advisor to public sector organizations, Intuition has practical experience solving learning design challenges and delivering high-performance knowledge solutions within the specific confines of the public sector.

Our breadth of experience spans projects with specialist public sector agencies to global inter-governmental organizations and NGO’s. In each case, Intuition delivers solutions that help transform and modernize service delivery. We do this through the distribution and management of knowledge and information, across multiple languages, often in challenging environments around the world.

Whether it is putting learning in the hands of a humanitarian worker in Sub-Saharan Africa or delivering field medical training to military personnel, Intuition has the end-to-end capabilities to meet a wide variety of knowledge requirements.
