Advisory Services

Building the blueprint for success

As the business landscape continues to change, the risk and reward of effective planning has reached critical levels. With organizations continuing to integrate new and existing systems, the need to effectively streamline and automate processes while also meeting the demands of the modern employee can be overwhelming.

Intuition works with organizations on a range of talent management concerns from on-boarding to learning strategies. Through expert advice and years of practical experience, Intuition works with customers to develop processes and programs that meet the talent and technology needs of the organization.

Our Advisory Services cover three main areas:

Learning Consultancy

From content development to assignment, delivery and reporting, Intuition’s learning consultants provide practical, industry-focused advice and recommendations on how best to maximize the impact and effectiveness of client’s learning strategies.

Taking a business-oriented approach, our Advisory team partner with organizations to:

  • Analyze the learning requirements from your business lines
  • Evaluate the existing business processes and content
  • Recommend the optimal blend of learning approaches, delivery channels and learner support to achieve your business goals
  • Conduct a training needs analysis (TNA) to assess and advise on the most suitable learning interventions and training initiatives

Technology Consultancy

With a global team of specialist technology consultants and developers, Intuition has built an unrivaled reputation for designing and implementing effective learning solutions which help organizations to unlock, manage and share their enterprise knowledge.

Our technology consultants engage with customers on focused short-term projects and strategic long-term engagements such as:

  • Mobile Learning and Knowledge Management implementations
  • Executing enterprise integrations ( SCORM, AICC, SSO) covering LMS/LCM’s
  • Providing Project Management for large-scale technology roll-outs
  • Developing Content Authoring and Delivery strategies
  • Producing technical and functional specifications for project charters and security reviews

Although each client engagement is defined by specific business goals and needs, Intuition works to streamline our solutions for clients through established capabilities, key deliverables, and managed risk.
